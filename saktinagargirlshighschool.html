Saktinagar Girls' High School



Saktinagar Girls'High School is one of the oldest schools in Krishnanagar, conveniently situated at Saktinagar, near Krishnanagar Railway Station and the District Hospital, Nadia. Established in 1949, this school ushered in a new era of enlightenment and all round progress for the aspiring young girls of the locality and also vindicated the relentless struggle and sacrifice of the people of the area. Beginning her journey as a 4class institution, the school was later upgraded to secondary and higher secondary levels in 1976 and 2010 respectively. Today Saktinagar Girls'High School boasts of her 1200+ student strength, sprawling three storey building, complete with a state of the art Auditorium, a multi-functional Computer lab, NCC senior and junior wings, a thrice State Champion Hockey team and a truly dedicated faculty and office staff.
