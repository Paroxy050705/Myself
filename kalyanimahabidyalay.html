Kalyani Mahabidyalay




Kalyani Mahavidyalaya, popularly known as Kalyani College established in 1999, is a government aided general degree college at Kalyani in Nadia district, West Bengal, India.[1] The college offers undergraduate degree courses in arts, science and commerce. History The college was established and started its journey on 18th of July, 1999.[2] The Institution was initially eligible to run only four Hons. courses and 2 pass courses, but from the year of 2000-2001 the University of Kalyani allowed the college to run 18 courses.[3] Dr. Amiya Kumar Mudi was the first principal of this college.[4] Departments Science Botany Mathematics Statistics Computer Science Molecular Biology and Biotechnology Microbiology Physiology Physics Chemistry Arts and Commerce Bengali English Education Economics History Geography Political Science Sociology Commerce Accreditation Kalyani Mahavidyalaya is affiliated to University of Kalyani[5] and is also recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC).[6] In 2018, the college was given B grade (Institutional CGPA:-2.08) by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council(NAAC).[7]
